01. About Me

In February 2021, I signed up for a short Intro to Coding course; this was how I discovered the world of code for the first time and fell in love with it completely.

In May 2022, I earned a Computer Software Engineering diploma from IPMC University College, Accra, Ghana.

Since then I have gotten certified in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, ReactJS, Ruby, and Ruby on Rails at Microverse Technical Bootcamp, San Francisco, USA, and I have built multiple web applications for my personal use, for family, friends, and even for paying clients as a freelancer on Upwork dot com.
